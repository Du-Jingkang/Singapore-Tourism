Singapore's History

Year 1299

Discovering Temasek

Sang Nila Utama discovers the fishing village of Temasek and renames it "Singapura".

28 February 1819

Arrival of the British

Sir Stamford Raffles arrives in Singapore and establishes it as a British trading port.

15 February 1942

Japanese Invades

The Japanese Occupation of Singapore during World War 2 begins after the British surrender.

9 August 1965

Singapore's Independence

Singapore gains independance from Malaysia.
